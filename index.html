    ThuroDry Emergency Flood Services in San Diego

    Since 1983, ThuroDry has specialized in emergency flood restoration services in San Diego and surrounding areas. Over the years, we have helped residents of the area deal with the fallout of major rainstorms, burst pipes, or any other cause of flooding. Our main branches of service include flood and water damage, sewage damage, structural drying, and insurance claims.

    San Diego Flood and Water Damage Services

    We focus on emergency water removal, water damage restoration, and odor and mold control. Bad weather and broken pipes can wreak all sorts of havoc in your San Diego home. We work to ensure that this damage not cause a bigger headache than what is necessary. If you wake up in the middle of the night to your basement or ground level floor completely flooded, you can pick up the phone and reach out to us right then and there. After we're done clearing out the water, we'll inspect the damage and work with you to agree on how and whether you want us to restore the damage done. After we've restored the damage, we can inspect and make sure there is no mold growth happening, and we can make sure you won't be breathing in the harmful odors.

    Sewage Damage Services in San Diego

    ThuroDry is standing by ready to help with any sewage damage services our clients in San Diego and surrounding areas may need. We specialize in sewage extraction - getting rid of all the unwanted debris that could be blocking and damaging your sewage. We also provide containment and HEPA filtration, making sure the sewage does not spill out and the HEPA filtration in order to ensure the water that is being recycled for us in your home is clean and usable.

    Structural Drying Services in San Diego

    We offer our clients in San Diego structural drying services for the aftermath of a flooding that has taken place in your home. Flooding can damage your walls and weaken the foundation of the home, leading to the danger of having the structure collapse with victims inside. We get into every possible location of the house, whether it's in the crawlspace, on the main floors for carpet and structural drying, or the de-humidification of the air to prevent that from damaging the walls.
